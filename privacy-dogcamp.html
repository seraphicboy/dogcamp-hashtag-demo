Privacy Policy – DogCamp Instagram Dashboard

Business: DogCamp Airport Branch (“DogCamp”, “we”, “our”)
App name: 공항점 인스타분석 (DogCamp Instagram Dashboard)
Last updated: 28 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how DogCamp Airport Branch (“DogCamp”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) uses data obtained from Meta platforms through our internal web application, 공항점 인스타분석 (DogCamp Instagram Dashboard). This app is an internal tool used only by DogCamp staff to connect and monitor our own Instagram Business account @dogcamp.gochon and our own Facebook Page 도그캠프 공항점.

1. Who we are

DogCamp Airport Branch is a pet hotel and dog kindergarten business in Korea. We operate the Instagram Business account @dogcamp.gochon and the Facebook Page 도그캠프 공항점 to share updates about our services and communicate with customers. This dashboard app is owned and operated by DogCamp and is not offered to the general public.

2. What data we access from Meta

Through the Meta API, our app requests the following permissions: instagram_basic, pages_show_list, pages_read_engagement, and Instagram Public Content Access.

Using these permissions, we access only the following types of data:

  • instagram_basic – basic information for our own Instagram Business account:
    • Instagram Business Account ID (id)
    • Username (username)
    • Total number of media posts (media_count)
  • pages_show_list – list of Facebook Pages that our own business admin account can manage:
    • Page ID (id)
    • Page name (name)
    We use this only to confirm and select our own Page 도그캠프 공항점.
  • pages_read_engagement – basic engagement metrics for our own Page posts:
    • Post impressions (post_impressions)
    • Post engaged users (post_engaged_users)
    These metrics are used only to understand which DogCamp posts perform better so we can improve our content.
  • Instagram Public Content Access – public posts that use our own brand/event hashtags, for example #도그캠프공항점, #2023트리샷이벤트, #2024트리샷이벤트, #2025트리샷이벤트. We only view posts that customers have voluntarily made public with these hashtags.

We do not post, edit, or delete any content on Instagram or Facebook via the API. We do not read or store direct messages, private comments, or private data from other users. We only access data for our own Business accounts and public content that customers choose to share with our brand/event hashtags.

3. How we use this data

The data obtained via Meta permissions is used only for the following purposes:

  • To verify which Instagram Business account and Facebook Page are connected to our internal dashboard
  • To display basic account information (ID, username, media count) to our staff
  • To view simple engagement metrics for our own DogCamp Page posts
  • To list public posts that use our own event hashtags so we can:
    • confirm which customers joined events like “트리샷이벤트”, and
    • optionally re-share some posts to our Instagram Stories with proper credit to the customer.

The app is an internal tool for DogCamp staff and is not accessible to customers or the general public. We do not use this data for third-party advertising networks or cross-app tracking.

4. Data storage and retention

Our current implementation runs as a simple web dashboard. Data from Meta (such as account IDs, usernames, media counts, Page IDs, and engagement metrics) is:

  • Requested from Meta APIs when a staff member logs in and opens the dashboard
  • Displayed directly in the browser for that session
  • Not permanently stored in any external database by this app

Access tokens used for the connection are handled according to Meta’s platform policies and are not shared with third parties.

5. Data sharing and third-party processors

We do not sell, rent, or share Meta platform data with any third-party companies or external service providers.

Meta platform data accessed through this app is used only by DogCamp staff for internal business purposes and is not provided to other businesses.

6. Children’s privacy

This app is an internal business tool and is not directed to children. It is used only by DogCamp staff members.

7. Security

We limit access to this dashboard to authorized DogCamp staff only. We follow Meta’s Platform Policies regarding access tokens and platform data. We do not intentionally collect or store sensitive personal information from customers via this app.
