Privacy Policy – DogCamp Instagram Dashboard

Business: DogCamp Airport Branch (“DogCamp”, “we”, “our”)
App name: 공항점 인스타분석 (DogCamp Instagram Dashboard)
Last updated: 28 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how DogCamp Airport Branch (“DogCamp”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) uses data obtained from Meta platforms through our internal web application, 공항점 인스타분석 (DogCamp Instagram Dashboard). This app is an internal tool used only by DogCamp staff to connect and monitor our own Instagram Business account @dogcamp.gochon.

1. Who we are

DogCamp Airport Branch is a pet hotel and dog kindergarten business in Korea. We operate the Instagram Business account @dogcamp.gochon to share updates about our services and to communicate with customers. This dashboard app is owned and operated by DogCamp and is not offered to the general public.

2. What data we access from Meta

Through the Meta API, our app requests the following permission: instagram_basic.

Using this permission, we only access basic information about our own Instagram Business account, specifically:

  • Instagram Business Account ID (id)
  • Username (username)
  • Total number of media posts (media_count)

Currently, our app calls only this read-only endpoint:

/{ig-user-id}?fields=id,username,media_count

We do not post, edit, or delete any content on Instagram via the API. We do not read or store direct messages, comments, or private data from other users. We only access basic information for our own Business account that we own and control.

3. How we use this data

The data obtained via instagram_basic is used only for the following purposes:

  • To verify which Instagram Business account is connected to our internal dashboard
  • To display basic account information (ID, username, media count) to our staff
  • To confirm that our connection to the Instagram Graph API is working correctly

The app is an internal tool for DogCamp staff and is not accessible to customers or the general public. We do not use this data for advertising networks or for cross-app tracking.

4. Data storage and retention

Our current implementation runs as a simple web dashboard. The Instagram Business account data (ID, username, media_count) is:

  • Requested from Meta APIs when a staff member logs in and opens the dashboard
  • Displayed directly in the browser for that session
  • Not permanently stored in any external database by this app

Access tokens used for the connection are handled according to Meta’s platform policies and are not shared with third parties.

5. Data sharing and third-party processors

We do not sell, rent, or share Meta platform data with any third-party companies or external service providers.

Meta platform data accessed through this app is used only by DogCamp staff for internal business purposes and is not provided to other businesses.

6. Future hashtag features

In the future, we plan to request Instagram Public Content Access in order to display public posts that contain our brand hashtags (for example, #도그캠프공항점 or #dogcamp).

Those hashtag features will use the Hashtag Search API and the Public Content Access permission. They will not rely on the instagram_basic permission.

Even for hashtag features, we only intend to view and organize public posts that customers choose to share with our brand hashtags, for the purpose of brand monitoring and customer engagement.

7. Children’s privacy

This app is an internal business tool and is not directed to children. It is used only by DogCamp staff members.

8. Security

We limit access to this dashboard to authorized DogCamp staff only. We follow Meta’s Platform Policies regarding access tokens and platform data. We do not intentionally collect or store sensitive personal information from customers via this app.
